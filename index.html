Who We Are

A proudly South African waste management company turning waste into wealth and powering a greener Africa.

Our Story

PhindaPhala was founded in 2025 on a simple but powerful principle: waste doesn't have to be the end of a product's journey. Our name, rooted in African heritage, means "to return" or "to multiply" — embodying the circular economy at its core.

We're not just a waste collection company — we're sustainability partners helping businesses, municipalities, and communities make smarter decisions about waste.

Modern waste sorting and recovery systems
A reliable green fleet for waste collection

A Message from Our Founder

Armstrong McBaye
Founder & CEO
McBaye Group Johannesburg, SA

"Waste doesn't have to be the end of a story. At PhindaPhala, we believe every discarded material holds untapped value — and it's our mission to unlock it for a better Africa."

Armstrong McBaye is a South African entrepreneur, logistics professional, and business leader serving as the Founder and CEO of McBaye Group — a diversified business group focused on environmental services, logistics, community development, and sport.

With a strong background in logistics and supply chain, Armstrong built his career around operational efficiency, strategic business development, and creating sustainable models that solve real-world challenges across Southern Africa. His experience spans vehicle exports, cross-border logistics, fleet coordination, and client relationship management — giving him deep insight into the movement of goods and services throughout the SADC region.

As the driving force behind PhindaPhala, Armstrong leads the strategic direction of an integrated environmental and facilities management company committed to operational excellence, sustainability, and community upliftment. He is also passionate about youth empowerment through Johannesburg FC, creating opportunities for young football talent while building a professional and community-driven institution.
